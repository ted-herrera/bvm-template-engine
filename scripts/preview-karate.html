About Champions Karate Academy

Champions Karate Academy has been teaching discipline, respect, and self-defense to Tulsa families for over 14 years. Founded by Sensei David Park, a 5th-degree black belt, we offer age-appropriate programs for kids ages 4 and up, teens, and adults. Our structured curriculum builds confidence, physical fitness, and life skills that extend far beyond the dojo.

Frequently Asked Questions

What age can my child start?

We accept students as young as 4 years old in our Little Champions program.

Do you offer a free trial class?

Yes — every new student gets a free introductory class with no obligation.

What style of martial arts do you teach?

We teach traditional Shotokan karate with modern self-defense applications.

How often should my child attend class?

We recommend 2-3 classes per week for consistent progress.

Do adults train separately from kids?

Yes — we have dedicated adult classes in the evenings.
