About Tony's Pizza

Tony's Pizza has been serving authentic New York style pizza to Tulsa since 2004. Owner Tony Moretti brought his family's recipes straight from Brooklyn — hand-tossed dough made fresh every morning, house-made sauce from San Marzano tomatoes, and whole-milk mozzarella pulled daily. No shortcuts. No frozen anything. Just real pizza the way it's supposed to be made.
